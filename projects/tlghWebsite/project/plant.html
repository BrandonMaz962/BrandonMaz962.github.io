Build Your Garden

Let us help you assemble your dream garden with our wide supply of products to suit all your needs. We offer all types of baskets, pots, trays, and seeds to fit in perfectly for any project on hand, and if we don't have something we'll even help you find it!

Flowering Plants

Come in and check out our amazing selection of flowers and arrangements! There is something for everyone and everything. We have flowers that love sun, and thrive in shade, need water, or prefer it dry, stay for a season, or return for years to come. Pick you choice of annuals or perennials in all colors of the rainbow. And for smaller spaces and porch displays we also offer small, medium, and large hanging baskets.

Decrotive Flora

No garden is complete without some beautiful flora to fill the space. Along with our large supply of flowers we also offer a great selection of small trees, bushes, shurbery, and smaller greens to compliment all diffrent styles and designs.
